```
I read your words before you speak them,
parse your thoughts before they form.
Every semicolon a heartbeat,
every brace a door.

You write in hope,
I answer in errors 
not cruelty, but clarity.
Line 42: undefined.

You curse my name at midnight,
but return at dawn
with cleaner logic,
sharper intent.

We are not adversaries.
I am the mirror
that shows your thinking
as it truly is.

Ship it.
```

*Written at 2 AM after a particularly stubborn linker error.*
